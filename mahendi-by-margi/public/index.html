Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the mehendi stain last on the skin?

The stain typically lasts from one to three weeks, depending on skin type, aftercare, and mehendi quality.

Is mehendi safe?

Yes, when natural mehendi is used. We use only natural mehendi, ensuring a safe and beautiful experience for you.

What designs available?

We offer a wide range of designs, from traditional to contemporary. We also create custom designs based on your preferences.

How do I book?

You can book an appointment through our website, by phone, or via email. We recommend booking in advance.
